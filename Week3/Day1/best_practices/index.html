A Volunteer's Day at UPNicaragua

Volunteering, a two-way road.

September 16, 2016 | Jasmin Samara

After learning about the struggles young Nicaraguan girls face when growing up in an environment that lacks opportunities and services fro them to become successful, I knew I wanted to be a part of a change for them.Today was my first experience with the young girls of UPNicaragua. After being acquainted with the other mentors of the program, I participated in a welcome exercise that would allow the girls to get to know me.
